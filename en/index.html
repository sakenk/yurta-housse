About the Yurt

Authentic Kazakh spirit in a modern format

Our yurt is a two-level (2 қабат) traditional Kazakh dwelling, beautifully decorated inside with carpets, ornaments and national décor. Here you will feel the true spirit of the steppe and mountains.

The yurt accommodates large groups — perfect for family outings, corporate events, birthday parties, Kazakh-style celebrations and any special occasion.

FAQ

Can we stay overnight? ▾
Yes, we welcome overnight guests. The yurt is warm and cosy even at night. We recommend booking in advance and paying a deposit to secure your date.
Can we bring our own food and drinks? ▾
Of course! You can bring your own food. We have a BBQ grill, kazan and samovar. We provide cookware. You can also pre-order Kazakh dishes — just ask when booking.
What's included in the rental — BBQ, cookware? ▾
The yurt rental includes: BBQ grill, kazan, samovar, necessary cookware, volleyball court and access to the grounds. Charcoal and firewood can be purchased from us or brought yourself.
Do you host large groups and corporate events? ▾
Yes! The yurt holds up to 30 people. We happily host corporate events, birthdays, Kazakh-style celebrations and any occasion. Write to us — we'll discuss the details.
How do I book and is a deposit required? ▾
Send us a WhatsApp message with your preferred date, number of guests and time. To confirm your booking we ask for a deposit. Contact us — we'll explain everything.
Can we come with children and pets? ▾
With children — absolutely! There's so much for them: horse riding, fresh air and nature. Regarding pets, please check in advance via WhatsApp.
